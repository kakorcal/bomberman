Players:

  • You: Bomberman
  • Enemy: creeper x 3

Rules:

  • Objective: Destroy the three creepers by exploding bombs near them
  • Win: All three enemies are destroyed
  • Lose: Get bombed by the creepers or by the bombs you've placed

Controls:

  • Arrow-up: Move up
  • Arrow-down: Move down
  • Arrow-right: Move right
  • Arrow-left: Move left
  • Spacebar: Drop bomb
